Our story

We're building the learning universe we wished our kids had.

Adey World began with a simple question: why is there so little world-class, joyful learning content that reflects African children and their languages? So we made it.

Our mission

Every child deserves to see themselves in what they learn — and to play their way into two languages at once.

Why we built it

Rooted in culture, built for the future

Most kids' apps are translations of the same Western characters and stories. We started from the opposite direction — original heroes like Adey, set in markets, savannas and family kitchens, with games that teach the Fidel alongside ABCs.

Underneath the fun is modern, AI-native technology that personalizes every child's path and lets us ship new worlds faster than anyone — all reviewed by qualified early-childhood educators before launch.
